The story

The name behind the portfolio

I stepped into life sciences marketing nearly three years ago without fully knowing what I was walking into. What I found was an industry unlike any other — one where the work genuinely matters, where clarity has real consequences, and where marketing sits at the intersection of science, strategy and human impact. It changed how I think about marketing entirely.

Axera Creative is the name I gave this portfolio because it reflects what I do rather than just who I am. "Ax" for precision and cutting through complexity. "Era" for a new chapter — because that's what this industry became for me. The "Creative" is intentional too — life sciences marketing isn't just technical, it demands creativity to make complex things land with the people who need to understand them.
